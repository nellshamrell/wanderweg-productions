"The Celtic Cross," Wanderweg Productions' first play, was conceIved while Matthew studied theatre in London and Ireland. After several trips to Belfast, he wrote Bryan this message:

"Hey. You’ll be in London next fall” (Matthew was there in the summer of ’08, Bryan in the fall) “and you’re gonna take a field trip up to Belfast and you’re gonna love it as much as I did. Do you wanna do a two-man show with me when we both get back in the spring of ’09?
Sweet.
Matthew”

Bryan wrote back shortly, saying something to this effect:

“Psh. Yeah.”
